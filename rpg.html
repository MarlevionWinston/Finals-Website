What are RPGs?

Role-Playing Games (RPGs) are a genre of video games where players assume the roles of characters in a fictional setting. Players take control of a character or party and engage in quests, battles, and exploration.

Characteristics of RPGs

  • Character Development
  • Storytelling and Narrative
  • Exploration of Open Worlds
  • Turn-Based or Real-Time Combat

Popular RPGs

Some of the most popular RPGs include:

  • The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt
  • Final Fantasy VII
  • Dark Souls
  • Persona 5
