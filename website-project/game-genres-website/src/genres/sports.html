Overview of Sports Games

The Sports genre encompasses video games that simulate the practice of sports. These games often feature real-life sports such as football, basketball, soccer, and more.

Characteristics

  • Realistic gameplay mechanics
  • Multiplayer options for competitive play
  • Licensing of real teams and players

Popular Sports Games

  • FIFA Series
  • Madden NFL Series
  • NBA 2K Series
  • WWE 2K Series

Competitive Aspects

Many sports games feature competitive modes, including online tournaments and leagues, allowing players to compete against each other globally.
